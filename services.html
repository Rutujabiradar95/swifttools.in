Services

Resharpening

We extend services of high quality resharpenings of all type of hobs, shaper cutters,form cutters, special application cutters, serration cutters etc. manintaining original class accuracies. Hob/Cutters of straight or helical flutes; radical, positive or negative rake; regular or staggered teeth with A or AA class quality. We have capacity from Mikron type hobs up to 20 Mod large tools.


Gear Hobbing

We do have capacity for hobbing of smaller gears in the range of 0.25 Mod and of any high helix angles and with high accuracies. We undertake batch quanitity hobbing. We have facilities for worm thread cutting up to 10 module and 1500 mm long with single or multi starts.
